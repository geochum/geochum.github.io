About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! I'm currently a Ph.D. Student specializing in Computational & Applied Mathematics. With a burning passion for unraveling complex mathematical problems and harnessing data, I aspire to become a Research/Data Scientist.

A profound love for mathematics has always fueled my academic journey. Beyond the theoretical aspects, I'm fascinated by the practical applications of math, especially in computational science. This fascination also extends to technology, making me a tech enthusiast, always looking for the latest advancements that can bridge the gap between mathematical theories and real-world challenges.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I take pride in being an experienced math educator. There's a unique joy in simplifying intricate concepts for budding minds, and I cherish the moments when I see understanding light up a student's face. Through teaching, I've refined my communication skills, ensuring that complex ideas are conveyed in an understandable and relatable manner.

Thank you for visiting, and feel free to explore the site to learn more about my projects, research, and other interests.
